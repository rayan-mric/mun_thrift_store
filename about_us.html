Our Story

Founded by Students

Margarita started this initiative to create a student-run thrift store at MUN, making sustainable shopping accessible and affordable for everyone on campus.

Struggle & Securing Space

Our team protested in the town hall and coordinated with space administration and the President’s office to secure a permanent room. After proper renovations, our thrift store is now operating successfully for the campus community.

Giving Back

The money we receive from yard sales is donated to the Campus Food Bank to make a better world. In Winter 2024, we collected and donated $2,314.80 to support students and community members in need.

How it Works

1. Donate

Students drop items during open hours.

2. Sort

Volunteers inspect quality.

3. Price

Affordable student pricing.

4. Reuse

Promote sustainability on campus.
